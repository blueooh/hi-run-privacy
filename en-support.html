Hi-Run Support

FAQ

Apple Watch is not connecting

Make sure your iPhone and Apple Watch are signed in with the same Apple ID. Also check that Hi-Run is installed on your Apple Watch via the Watch app on your iPhone.

Heart rate is not being recorded

On your Apple Watch, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Hi-Run and make sure permissions are enabled.

I want to delete a record

In the History tab, swipe left on the record you want to delete.

I want to restore my purchase

Tap the "Restore Purchase" button on the purchase screen. If you previously purchased with the same Apple ID, it will be restored automatically.
